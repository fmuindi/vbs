Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before registering. Can't find your answer? Call us at (614) 837-1990.

Do parents stay during VBS?
No — this is a drop-off event. Our background-checked, trained team is ready to welcome and care for your kids so you can enjoy a night off!
What should my child wear?
Comfortable clothes and closed-toe shoes for active games. Kids will be jumping, running, and having an absolute blast every night.
Is there food?
Yes! Light snacks and drinks are provided each night. Please note any food allergies during registration so we can accommodate your child.
Can I register multiple kids?
Absolutely! Register all your children in one go at whc.life/vbs. Bring the whole crew — the more the merrier!
What grades are eligible?
VBS is designed for children currently in 1st through 5th grade with age-appropriate activities and Bible lessons for each group.
Is registration really free?
100% free! World Harvest Church covers all costs so every family can participate. No hidden fees — just bring your kids!
