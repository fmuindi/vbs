Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before registering your child. Can't find an answer? Call us at (614) 837-1990.

Do parents stay during VBS?
No, this is a drop-off event. Our team is background-checked, trained, and ready to welcome and care for your kids while you enjoy a night out!
What should my child wear?
Comfortable clothes and closed-toe shoes suitable for active games and activities. Kids will be moving, jumping, and having tons of fun!
Is there food?
Yes! Light snacks and drinks will be provided each night. If your child has food allergies, please note them during registration.
Can I register multiple kids?
Absolutely! You can register all your children during the same registration process at whc.life/vbs. Bring the whole crew!
What grades are eligible?
VBS is designed for children currently in 1st through 5th grade. We have age-appropriate activities and Bible lessons for each group.
Is registration really free?
100% free! World Harvest Church covers all costs so every family can participate. No hidden fees, no donations required — just bring your kids!
